COMPANY PACKAGING AND RESPONSIBILITY

Goods made from sustainable materials often have a longer lifespan, but when it does reach the end of its life, it also means it’s easier to dispose of it sustainably. That’s why we prioritize sourcing materials that are readily biodegradable or compostable, like natural fibers, bio-based formulas, paper, or eternally recyclable, like aluminum, stainless steel, brass.

FEEDING THE NATION, REDUCING HUNGER

One of out main priorities at Sustainable Growth Partners is to ensure that we reduce any levels of hunger across the wrold by donating as much as we can through our natural produce. We have invested 1 million in ensuring that every single chid across Africa has access to a balanced diet.




HOW WE FEED THE NATION

We take time as a company to go out into the fields and plant all of the crops needed for a heatlhy meal. We have spend 50 years providing food to those in need to ensure that no one goes hungry. We have a goal of reducing famine across countries in Africa and Asia.





80% Decline in Famine

In 2020, the company was able to reduce famine in war countries by 80% saving more than 16 000 lives of children who has been going through malnutrition.




25% Increase in Volunteers

Sustainable Grwoth Partners has reached a record of 20 000 volunteers who have spent their time farming and harvesting crops used to feed people.
